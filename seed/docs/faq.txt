Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I track my order?
A: Use the order tracking page with your order number and email.

Q: Can I change my shipping address after ordering?
A: Yes, within 2 hours of placing the order via customer support.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: Credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise accounts.

Q: How do I escalate a Tier-2 ticket?
A: Mark the ticket as "escalate" in the support portal and a supervisor will review within 4 hours.

Q: Do you offer volume discounts?
A: Yes, enterprise pricing is available for orders over $10,000 annually.
